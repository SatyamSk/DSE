About Us

Redefining Commerce
Education Since 1967

The Department of Commerce, Faculty of Commerce and Business, Delhi School of Economics, University of Delhi, traces its origins to December 1967. Established as a separate entity, it has imbibed the DSE tradition of exploring new frontiers of knowledge and innovation. Over more than five decades, it has redefined commerce education in India.

The MBA (International Business) programme prepares students to navigate the complexities of global markets — combining rigorous academics, industry exposure, and a vibrant campus culture to produce well-rounded business leaders.
